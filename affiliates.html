FAQ
Common questions
Who can be an affiliate? +
Anyone! Marketers, consultants, accountants, web designers, real estate agents, or anyone who works with service businesses. If you know people who run businesses that rely on phone calls, this is for you.
How do I track my referrals? +
You'll get a unique referral link and access to a simple dashboard that shows clicks, signups, and your commission balance in real-time.
When and how do I get paid? +
Commissions are paid monthly via PayPal or Zelle, 30 days after the referred customer's payment clears. No minimum payout threshold.
Do I need to do the sales pitch? +
Nope. Just share your link or make an introduction. We handle the demo, the pitch, the setup — everything. You just connect us with the business owner.
How long does the commission last? +
As long as the customer stays subscribed. If they're a customer for 3 years, you earn commission for 3 years. There's no expiration.
Is there a limit on how many people I can refer? +
No cap whatsoever. Refer 5 or refer 500 — you earn on every single one.
